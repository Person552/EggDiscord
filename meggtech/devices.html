Devices

This is a list of meggtech’s devices and their functions.

  • Healer - Can heal most wounds after charging.

  • Cloaking Device - Can hide someone's physical form with the click of a button.

  • Pain’r Brain’r - Come in pairs, one dominant, once the dominant one's user thinks of a chosen word or image, the other one's user will be shocked.

  • Boiler Boom - Makes the user immune to the Pain'r Brain'r and other mind manipulating devices.

  • Roper - Upon a hard contact it shoots ropes out from it's sides, entangling whatever it hits.

  • MeggPhone - A supercharged phone.

  • MaggPhone - A normal phone.

  • Eggcubator - Can change any gene of a child in a mother's womb, also provides perfectly healthy and nutritious food, water and air.

  • EggBeggar - Any gifted egg in contact with the device will be unable to use their power.

  • Gluer - Will secrete glue from itself.

  • Flashbeargg - Will force anybody who it touches to keep touching it.

  • Power Drainer - Can drain entire cities worth of electricity in seconds.

  • Polite Goodbye - Will explode after anybody says "AWWWWW" in its vicinity.
